Professional Biography

Hi, I'm Damon.

I'm a Full Stack Developer based in Kenya, specialising in building high-performance, scalable web applications and distributed systems. I've spent the last 3 years working across startups and enterprise organisations, leading engineering teams and architecting systems that serve millions of users.

My journey started with a passion for understanding how things work at a systems level — from bare-metal performance optimisation to elegant API design. Today, I bring that same rigour to every layer of the stack, whether I'm designing a mysql schema, javascript, or refining a React component's micro-interactions.

I believe software engineering is as much a discipline of communication as it is of code. The best systems are built by teams with shared context, clear abstractions, and a relentless commitment to quality.
